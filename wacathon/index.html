WAC SPRINT

Have you ever wished that you could share your ideas for reinventing education? Join us at the inaugural WAC: SPRINT! Collaborate with other passionate students to develop unique ideas to reimagine education. Engage with the team from Future Design School as you develop your ideas, validate them and craft a pitch that you will present to a panel of experts judges for great prizes!

SPRINT is a competitive design sprint, separate from the regular activities on the day of WAC. After a five hour session, it will culminate in a pitch competition in front of a panel of top educators and industry professionals across Toronto. We are proud to announce that this panel will be chaired by Michele Romanow, one of Canada’s top tech entrepreneurs and Dragon on the hit show, Dragon’s Den.
