WAC SPRINT

Have you ever wished that you could share your ideas for reinventing education? Join us at the inaugural WAC: SPRINT! Collaborate with other passionate students to develop unique ideas to reimagine education. Engage with the team from Future Design School as you develop your ideas, validate them and craft a pitch that you will present to a panel of experts judges for great prizes! Do note however the price of attendance will be $50 (Lunch will be provided.)
